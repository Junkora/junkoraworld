How to Play

  • MovementWASD or Arrow keys
    Explore the wasteland grid.
  • Tools1 Hoe • 2 Water • 3 Hand
    Press number keys to switch tools.
  • InteractE key
    Use your current tool on the tile in front of you.
  • FarmingTill • Plant • Water • Harvest
    Till soil with Hoe, click seed in inventory to equip, plant with Hand, water to grow.
  • GrowthReal-time
    Crops only progress while watered. Harvest with Hand when mature.
  • GatheringDecor nodes
    Stand near trees/flowers/minerals and click the green gather icon or press E (Hand).
  • InventoryBottom bar
    Seeds and items appear in slots. Click a seed to equip for planting.
  • SkillsProgression
    Mining/Flower/Harvesting level up as you gather and farm.
